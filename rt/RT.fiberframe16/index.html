About Me

Sebastian's career as both writer and actor has been in full flight for over eight years. From iconoic Birtish dramas such as "skins" and "The Borgias" to movies ranging from "Plastic" to "Kids in Love". Seb is a compulsive writer and is dedicated to creating a platoform where everyone has equal access to the film industry.
